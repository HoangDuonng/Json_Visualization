    JSON Visualization is an online tool designed to visualize and analyze various data formats, including JSON, YAML, CSV, XML and more.     The Draw view is a powerful whiteboard-style canvas that lets you visualize your JSON data as hand-drawn diagrams. It automatically converts your data structure into interactive nodes and arrows, giving you a more visual and creative way to explore relationships in your data. You can also freely draw, annotate, and rearrange elements on the canvas.
    You can switch between views using the toggle buttons at the top of the editor. The Graph view provides a traditional structured layout, while the Draw view offers a free-form whiteboard experience where you can interact with your data visually.
